| Business | $249/mo | 249 EUR/mo | Up to 7 + blog | 2-4 weeks | Priority |

### What every plan includes
- Custom design (no templates)
- Hosting on Vercel (free tier)
- Standard .com domain (~$12/yr, I absorb this)
- English + Spanish
- Mobile-first
- Basic SEO

### What is NOT included

### Commitment
- 12 months minimum
- After 12 months: client can keep at lower maintenance rate or own the site outright
- Early cancellation: case by case, but the commitment is what keeps the monthly price low
